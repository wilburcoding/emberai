Our Inspiration

From 2001 to 2020, the United States has averaged an incredible 68,707 wildfires annually—and those fires don’t just stay put. If you remember the Canadian wildfires of 2023, you’ll know the smoke didn’t need a passport to drift right on over to New York, rendering everything with a “Breaking Bad” filter. Meanwhile, out on the West Coast, LA fires have been making headlines again, reminding us that wildfires can ignite anywhere, any time. Seeing the widespread devastation and the ever-increasing number of wildfires firsthand inspired us to create a dynamic model and an easy-to-use website. We wanted to empower communities with the tools they need to understand their wildfire risk and take proactive steps to protect their homes and loved ones.
